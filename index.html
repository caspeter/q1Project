Read my Face

Read my Face is designed to help people recognize emotions on a person's face. You place the image URL (image address) into the input field and click submit to recieve your results. The emotions that are recognized are happiness, sadness, surprise, anger, fear, contempt, disgust and neutral.

Read my Face can recognize 64 faces in one image. If a face is obstructed in any way, it is not guarenteed that Read my Face will recognize that face or display emotions. Image files that are accpeted are JPEG, PNG, GIF(the first frame) and BMP. Files must be less than 4MB. The emotions contempt and disgust are experimental.

Made using Microsoft Cognitive Emotion API
